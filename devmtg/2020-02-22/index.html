Women and Compilers and Tools Workshop at CGO 2020

The first ever Women in Compilers and Tools Workshop at CGO 2020!

When: Febuary 22, 2020

Where: San Diego, CA

The workshop is co-located with CC, HPCA, and PPoPP. It takes place at Hilton San Diego Resort and Spa in San Diego, CA.

Register: At the CGO website.

About

This workshop is aimed to gather women and allies in the field of compilers and tools. There will be speakers,panels, and open converstaion about important topics relevant to increasing the diversity within compilers and tools and supporting women in the community.

As the agenda is finalized, we will publish the details here.
