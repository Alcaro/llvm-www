  • What: LLVM at FOSDEM.
  • When: January 30th, 2016
  • Where: Brussels, Belgium

At FOSDEM 2016, LLVM will participate with a dedicated devroom. Complementing the upcoming Euro LLVM 2016, the devroom at FOSDEM provides a great opportunity for core LLVM developers and the wider open source community to get together to connect and discuss.

As possibly the largest European Open Source Conference, FOSDEM takes place in Brussels and attracts with more than 400 lectures every year over 5000 hackers - many core contributors of the worlds leading open source projects.

We also invite you to sign up for the official Developer Meeting mailing list to be kept informed of updates concerning the meeting.

The full schedule will be published in January, 2016.

Logistics

The mailing list llvm-devroom@lists.fosdem.org can be used to discuss issues of general interest related to the conference organization.

Registration

FOSDEM does not require any registration and is free of charge. However, just like last year, an important crowd must be expected.
